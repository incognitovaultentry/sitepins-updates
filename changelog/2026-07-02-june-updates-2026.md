- **Save as Draft in CMS** — You can save draft without triggering a full rebuild, so your changes are stored instantly inside the app only.
- **Preferred LLM Input** — Manually choose/input your preferred LLM model from any of our four supported providers: OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, or Google Gemini.
- **Advanced Global Search** — Global search now covers organizations, user settings, organization settings, preferences, support, and account settings, not just files.
- **6-Digit OTP** — Email one-time passwords upgraded from 4 to 6 digits for stronger account security.
